PMS Connection

Auto-pull data from your PMS

A Computer Use Agent logs into your PMS exactly like a human would - navigating the screens, reading your occupancy and checkout data, and feeding it directly into Staxis. Zero manual entry.

Syncs every 15 minutes during operating hours (6 AM – 10 PM)

"Tomorrow Lock" sync at 9 PM - sends you tomorrow's recommended schedule

Morning confirmation sync at 5:30 AM for any overnight changes

Push notification when occupancy changes by 5+ rooms

The URL your staff uses to log in to the PMS

Your credentials are encrypted and stored securely in Supabase. They are only used by the Staxis sync agent to read occupancy data - never shared or sold.

How It Works

1

A headless browser opens your PMS at the scheduled sync time

2

The agent logs in with your saved credentials

3

It navigates to the occupancy/reservations screen and reads the data

4

Extracted data (rooms occupied, checkouts, check-ins) is saved to Staxis

5

If numbers changed significantly, you get a push notification

Currently supports Choice Advantage with full automation. Other systems use screenshot + OCR fallback.
